Solution:Living in Harmony with Nature:A Sustainable Future for Green Hills

At Green Hills, urban is about living in with nature. We are committed to preserving the beauty and bio-diversity of our while creating a sustainable for generations to come. Our vision is to establish a city that supports green space and environmental protection, while promoting smart growth and economic development. We in creating clean energy sources, investing in public transportation, reducing waste and water pollution, prioritizing public health. By taking a holistic approach to urbanism, we can ensure that Green Hills remains a vibrant, green city for years to come.

Construction at Green Hills will Start begining summer 2023
Green Hills is set to be one of the most advanced and modern developments in the region. With 14 m width for the main roads and 10 m width for secondary roads.

All roads within the development will be equipped with wide sidewalks on both sides of the road, as well as dedicated bicycle areas that will ensure safe and easy access for cyclists.

An advanced underground electrical system that utilises solar energy will power the development, eliminating unsightly overhead power lines and providing an uninterrupted visual appeal.

Additionally, the entrance of Green Hills Armenia is sure to be a spectacular sight, with its 50 meter wide opening creating a grand sense of arrival. Furthermore, there will be gas pipes and water pipes installed underground to ensure secure provision of both utilities.

Fiber Optics internet

Fiber optics technology will also be used throughout the project for fast internet connections. All these resources combined make Green Hills Armenia a unique infrastructure in its class that stands out from other developments in Armenia and beyond. The project is also designed to be resilient and sustainable, ensuring that residents will enjoy a safe and comfortable living experience for many years to come.

#Greenwifi

#greenwifi initiative provides residents with a comprehensive network of free wireless internet, giving them access to the digital world at no extra cost. This innovative initiative has been implemented in order to meet the needs of modern day living and provide users with an efficient way to stay connected.

Pedestrian Side Walks

Solar lights are an important component of Green Hills Armenia's commitment to sustainability and providing a modern living experience for its residents. Along the roads and pathways throughout the development, solar-powered lighting will be installed – efficiently and responsibly illuminating areas for pedestrians and cyclists.

Car Charging Stations

Green Hills will be equipped with a comprehensive car charging system, providing residents with convenient access to recharge their vehicles. Each charging station will have multiple connectors, allowing for simultaneous charging of multiple cars at once.

Furthermore, the system will allow for fast-charging services, meaning cars can be recharged within a short period of time – perfect for those who need a quick boost on the go!

Station N: Green

The Green Hills will implement an efficient electrical bus service that runs from Yerevan to Green Hills three times a day. This initiative is part of their ongoing commitment to sustainability and provides the citizens with reliable public transportation while reducing the environmental impact of traditional combustion engines. This technology has helped reduce the project's overall carbon footprint while providing a convenient way for residents to get around.

Natural Source for Irrigation

Every house will be equipped with an advanced wastewater reclamation system that filters and purifies wastewater before reusing it for agriculture, reducing the amount of water used from the environment. The project has also implemented rainwater harvesting systems throughout the green spaces, providing a natural source of irrigation for gardens and public areas.

Recycling Program

The project's garbage collection system is designed to minimise environmental impact as all waste is collected and sorted before being sent off for proper disposal or recycling. This initiative focuses on separating biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste and ensures that anything that can be recycled is done so, minimising the amount of waste that ends up in landfills.

Station N: Green

Green Hills has integrated green lights into the pedestrian areas of the development to further its commitment to sustainability. These energy-efficient lighting sources require up to 80% less energy than traditional light bulbs, producing a soft, natural glow that doesn’t create glare or light pollution. The green lights also provide an additional layer of security for pedestrians, illuminating pathways and making it easier for residents to identify any potential dangers in their surroundings.

Green Hills - A state-of-the-art eco-friendly housing project in Armenia, committed to creating a peaceful and harmonious living environment through careful zoning regulations, special parking regulations, comprehensive security system and efficient internal Regulations.

Construction Built up ratio at Green Hills

Green Hills is known for its thoughtful zoning regulations, which have been crafted to maximize the beauty of each residence. All homes are built with generous set-backs, ensuring that each property enjoys spectacular views and ample space between dwellings. At the same time, construction height regulations have been implemented in order to keep each home at an optimal distance from its neighbors, creating a peaceful and harmonious living environment.

A tree for Every 60 m of Land

Green hills has implemented an internal landscaping rule that requires one tree to be planted for every sixty meters of land. On top of practical benefits, planting trees creates a more inviting environment for tenants, staff, and visitors alike as they meander through the city. By increasing the amount of green space available at Green Hills, we hope to make it a more pleasant experience for all who enter its gates.

Special Parking Zoning

Green Hills has implemented special zoning regulations for car parking to ensure that the beauty of its residential areas is not compromised. All cars must be parked in designated parking lots or along streets within a certain distance from homes, and no vehicle can be left unattended on private or public property. This way, the project ensures that residential areas remain clean and cosy for its inhabitants.

Comprehensive Security System

Green Hills has implemented a comprehensive security system to ensure the safety of its residents. To monitor public spaces, the project has installed CCTV cameras across the roads and walkways, providing 24-hour surveillance and allowing authorities to quickly respond to any suspicious activity or incidents. Additionally, trained security personnel are stationed in key areas of the development, such as entrances and exits, to provide additional safety when needed.

Maintenance

Green Hills has implemented several strategies to ensure that the maintenance fees are lower than those of any other project. The project has integrated a range of green energy technologies, such as solar panels, into the design of all homes and public spaces. This renewable energy source is cheaper and more efficient than conventional sources, helping to reduce the cost of electricity and water bills. Additionally, the project has developed an efficient waste management system to minimise the amount of waste going into landfills, helping to keep maintenance costs low.
