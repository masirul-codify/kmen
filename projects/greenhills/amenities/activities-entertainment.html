Green Hills is a luxurious residential development located 15 minutes away from Yerevan. With its stunning views, spacious Villas, and first-class amenities.

Green Hills offers an unbeatable lifestyle for those seeking quality living. Residents will enjoy access to a fully equipped gym with yoga studio, heated indoor outdoor pool with sundeck, outdoor ice Skating, fishing lakes. Additionally there are several green spaces perfect for walking your dog or having a picnic lunch. All these features make Green Hills truly stand out from other developments in this area.

Activities

The Gym

The Green Hills gym is a state of the art facility with all the latest equipment. Not only does it provide a great space for working out, but it also offers a stunning view of the pristine lake next to it. The gym includes cardio machines, strength-training equipment, and free weights. It also has a dedicated yoga studio where professionals can lead classes and help maximize fitness goals. Additionally, its floor-to-ceiling windows overlook the beautiful lake, providing an amazing backdrop to any workout session. With calm waters and plenty of birdsong in the air, it's easy to find motivation here. And when finished with their workout routine, visitors can take a stroll around the lake or go for a swim in its crystal clear waters.

Yoga Classes

The outdoor yoga classes at Green Hills provide an unbeatable atmosphere to practice and relax. With stunning views of the lake, combined with fresh air and natural sounds, it's easy to find a sense of peace and focus here. Professionals lead the classes, offering a range of styles from traditional Hatha to more modern moves such as Vinyasa. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, the classes are designed to suit all levels and ensure that everyone leaves feeling relaxed and refreshed.

The Gathering "Venue"

The pool at Green Hills is truly unique. It starts indoors, but continues outdoors with a stunning view of the Araler mountain. It's heated to a comfortable temperature all year round, allowing residents to swim regardless of the season. The outdoor area features a large deck with sun loungers and umbrellas for soaking up the rays, as well as an infinity edge that overlooks the mountain range in the distance. During summer months, there's also plenty of space for children to splash around in shallow waters or play games on the tiled decking area. At night, the pool is illuminated by soft lighting that reflects off the water creating a tranquil atmosphere.

The pool area also includes several Jacuzzis where guests can relax and unwind after a long day exploring Yerevan city. Guests can enjoy spectacular views from every angle and take in breathtaking scenes of rolling hills and lush greenery. After dark, visitors can gaze up at star-filled skies or admire twinkling city lights below. With its perfect combination of relaxing amenities and invigorating surroundings, it's no wonder why this pool has become such a popular spot for residents and visitors alike!

The Gathering "Events & Weddings

During the summer months, Green Hills will be transformed into a vibrant entertainment venue as it hosts some of the best international artists from around the world. From jazz musicians to DJs and hip hop groups, there is something for everyone's music taste. The lush green setting provides an idyllic backdrop for the events.

Green Hills is the perfect venue for your special day. The romantic setting, breathtaking views and delicious cuisine make it a venue like no other. With outdoor spaces, large windows and plenty of seating inside, this restaurant is perfect for wedding guests to mingle in style.

Green Hills plays host to several summer events throughout the season, featuring some of the top international artists from around the globe. From live music concerts to pool parties, there's something for everyone at these events. During the summer months, guests can soak up the sun while enjoying delectable food and drinks, as well as a variety of activities

The experienced team at Green Hills are more than happy to help design the perfect event, taking care of every detail so that you can have a stress-free and enjoyable day. Whether it’s an intimate gathering or a large celebration, they will work with you to create the ideal atmosphere for your special occasion.



The Italian Signature Restaurant

The Italian Signature Restaurant at Green Hills offers a truly unique dining experience. With its elegant décor and Italian-inspired menu, the restaurant provides a taste of Italy right in Yerevan. The knowledgeable chefs use only the freshest ingredients to craft classic Italian dishes such as risotto, gnocchi, and carbonara. However, they also offer some innovative twists on traditional recipes, giving diners a chance to experience something new.

In addition to its delicious food, the venue also features an impressive wine list with over 400 labels from around the world. Guests can choose from a variety of reds and whites to enhance their meal or enjoy by itself. For those looking for an even more tailored experience, there are several sommeliers on hand to help select the perfect tipple.

The atmosphere at the Italian Signature Restaurant is warm and inviting; guests can enjoy intimate conversations by candlelight or admire breathtaking views of Araler mountain outdoors through floor-to-ceiling windows. There's also live entertainment every Friday including jazz performances, opera singers, and more. The ambiance makes it easy to forget you're in Yerevan and feel transported to another world!

At Green Hills' Italian Signature Restaurant, you'll find all the charm of Italy without ever having to leave Armenia! With its exquisite cuisine, extensive wine selection, and breathtaking views; it's no wonder why this restaurant has become one of Yerevan's most popular dining destinations.



Happiness in Every Step!

International Events

During the summer months, guests can enjoy spectacular performances from jazz musicians, DJs, hip hop groups and many more! The lush green setting provides an idyllic backdrop for these events and with plenty of space to dance, guests can truly immerse themselves in the music and take in breathtaking views of Araler mountain.

Green Hills is also home to an outdoor ice skating rink that is open all year round. This impressive feature provides a unique opportunity for guests to enjoy the thrill of ice skating in Yerevan’s picturesque surroundings. The arena is designed to cater for skaters of all abilities and levels, with different areas dedicated to beginners, intermediate skaters and those looking to take their skills up a notch.

The Outdoor Parks

Green Hills will be the home to some of the most stunning outdoor parks in Armenia. Here, residents can take in the lush green scenery and breathtaking views of Araler mountain , Ararat Montain and Yerevan view while enjoying free #greenwifi. Whether you're looking to get some exercise or simply relax, these parks have something for everyone. With plenty of benches, tables and chairs to choose from, you can enjoy a peaceful lunch or an evening stroll with friends and family.

Ice Skating

Green Hills is the perfect place to enjoy an unforgettable winter experience. The outdoor ice skating rink will allow residents to indulge in the thrill of gliding across the ice while taking in breathtaking views of Araler mountain Ararat and Yerevan city. The rink is ideal for skaters of all levels, with different sections dedicated to beginners, intermediate skaters and those looking to challenge themselves further.
