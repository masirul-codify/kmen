Planting the Seeds of Success

Welcome to the wonderful world of learning at Green Hills Kindergarten! We will hire experienced teachers and staff that will provide a nurturing environment for your little one to explore, create and grow.

Green Hills Project's Kindergarten is a unique and exciting place for children to learn, grow, and explore.

The kindergarten features a wide range of activities designed to help children develop their physical, social, intellectual, and emotional skills. The program encourages creativity and critical thinking through play-based learning opportunities. With trained and experienced teachers who understand the importance of building positive relationships with students, Green Hills Project's Kindergarten provides an optimal learning environment for young minds.

Green Hills Project's Kindergarten provides a variety of activities for students to explore and participate in, including:


  • Art projects
  • Drama and music classes
  • Storytelling sessions
  • Cooking and baking activities
  • Outdoor play and field trips
  • Physical education classes
  • Problem solving activities
  • Field trips to local attractions

Green Hills Project's Kindergarten has many great outdoor play activities for students, including:



  • Gardening and planting
  • Bubble blowing
  • Playing hopscotch or foursquare
  • Running obstacle courses
  • Water play in an inflatable pool
  • Soccer, basketball, and other sports games
  • Scavenger hunts and nature walks
  • Making a game out of everyday items
