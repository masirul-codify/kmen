GBC, the first buisness center with Hightech Features outside Yerevan

The GBC Top of the Art business center is designed to be a state-of-the art complex, with all necessary facilities and amenities available to meet the needs of its tenants.

The construction project is set to start in 2028 and will take into account international standards and specifications. It will host multi-national companies in technology, as well as other offshore corporations.

The GBC - Provides a wide range of features to support entrepreneurs who are just starting out or established business owners looking for an upgrade. Some of the options available are:

  • Fully-equipped workspace with high speed internet, computers, and phones for conveniently managing your business operations.
  • Access to meeting rooms where you can hold business meetings and collaborate with colleagues.
  • Audio-visual equipment for presentations, virtual events, and more.
  • Professional reception services to greet clients or customers and assist you in managing your business needs.
  • Onsite printing and copying services to help you handle important documents quickly and efficiently.
  • A private mailbox for secure storage of confidential mail or documents.
  • Mail forwarding services so that clients can reach you even when you're on the go.
  • Shredding services to ensure your confidential information remains secure.
  • Easy access to networking events where you can make valuable connections with potential partners or investors.
  • Administrative support staff who are available to assist with any queries or provide advice whenever you need it.
  • A virtual office service allowing you to maintain a professional presence from anywhere in the world.
  • Secure online storage so that all of your important business documents are securely backed up and accessible from anywhere.
