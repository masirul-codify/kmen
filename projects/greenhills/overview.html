Greenhills location and views

Green Hills offers breathtaking views of some of Armenia's most iconic landmarks. The development is located on a flat mountain top two kilometers away from the Achtarak M1 highway, offering a 360 degree view of Yerevan, Ararat Mountain and Valley, Ashtarak CIty, Yeghvard Town, and Araler Mountain. Every piece of land in Green Hills is guaranteed to have an incredible view of one of these amazing destinations. Green Hills Will be the trendsetter for innovation and modern infrastructure in Armenia

Green Hills is a city made with innovation and modern infrastructure in mind. Its gas, electricity, fiber optic internet connection, and water pipes are all secured beneath the surface to preserve the stunning natural environment. For convenience, car electrical charging points have been installed at 10 locations around the project. There are also restaurants and bars, a solar-powered electricity grid, swimming pools and lakes, an international school onsite and a one-of-a-kind business center for entrepreneurs... [learn more about Green Hills infrustructure ]

Green Hills follows a strict code for all built-up areas, landscaping, parking lots, and set-backs between plots.

This comprehensive code was custom-made by the renowned architecture firm A20, and its implementation will be registered in the cadaster of the Republic of Armenia. All residents of Green Hills will benefit from a pleasant aesthetic and enjoyable experience as they pass through the district. This code also ensures that property values remain consistent throughout the area, as well as providing necessary green spaces and lush landscaping for the enjoyment of all. With these guidelines in place, Green Hills provides a safe and attractive environment for people of all ages. Residents will be able to easily find parking spots and walkways, as well as enjoy the beauty of the area's natural environment. Furthermore, the code ensures that all properties are built to high-standards of safety and quality - making Green Hills an ideal place to live and work. With its own set of regulations, Green Hills is a great example of urban planning done right. The team at A20 have done an impressive job of creating a sustainable and attractive landscape that is sure to be appreciated by all who live here.
